About the Tournament

Welcome to the annual University of Washington Golf Tournament! This event is a great opportunity for alumni, students, faculty, and friends of the University to come together for a fun and competitive day of golf. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or just looking to enjoy a day on the greens, this tournament is perfect for all skill levels.
